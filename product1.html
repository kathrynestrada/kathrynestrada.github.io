Skincare Tools

Using tools in your skincare routine can immerse the product deeper into the skin for the best results. Tools can not only enhance your skin but also the products you are using. We currently offer our Gentle Exfoliating Sponge, Bamboo Facial Roller and Multi-Purpose Facial Brush. Our Gentle Exfoliating Sponge is great to pair with our Hydrating Foaming Cleanser to further clean your skin. The Bamboo Facial Roller is great to use when you want to increase blood circulation in the skin and have products like our Luminous Serum or Light-weight Moisturizer penetrate the skin. Lastly, the Multi-Purpose Facial Brush works wonderfully when applying our Light-weight Moisturizer or Exfoliating Mud Mask. Make sure to clean all skincare tools after each use.
