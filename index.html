About Us

At Carefree Beauty it is our main goal to provide clean skincare and makeup products that both you and your skin will love. We strive to create the best products possible by making our products with the best ingedients and free of animal-testing. We aim to provide our customers with a "carefree" approach to skincare and beauty products. Instead of searching for products that are cruelty-free, good for the environment and made with the best ingredients, we guarantee that all our products are clean and cruelty-free. When creating clean beauty, we eliminate the use of fragrances, dyes, alcohol and a variety of other harmful ingredients commonly found in beauty products. Our products are made to fit the needs of various skin types so whether you have sensitive, oily, dry or normal skin, look no further for healthy and radiant skin.

Our Purpose

We are motivated by what the positive impact good ingredients, products and methods can have on your skin. We also love seeing the progress individuals have as far as their skincare journey and we are proud to be apart of so many individual's journeys to love the skin they are in. We are happy to help so many from beginners to skincare to professionals and excited to share our handcrafted formulas with you in our products.
