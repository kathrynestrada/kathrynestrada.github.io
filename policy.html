Notice

The collection, use and sharing of all information collected through the Carefree Beauty website is determined by the customer. It is up to the user to input information they would want in our system, data collection is also limitied to what is relevant about the customer to better their experience while on the site and in the future with our company. Carefree Beauty does not associate any data to individual user identities. Any and all customer files may be permanently upon request of the customer.

Data Collection and Sharing

Carefree Beauty can collect a customer's name, email, phone number, date of birth, shipping and billing address and payment information. Browsing history within the site and frequently visited products are also collected as data to study the habits of users within our website. A customer can opt out of any data collection they may not want to provide and all information inputed within checkout is secure and confidential. Data is stored to better the expereince of customer's using our website in the future, to recommend products and give our team feedback for future launches. All information collected is up to user discretion and is not shared with any outside parties. It is prohibited by law to sell or distribute any personal information of our customers to any outside parties or companies.

Polcity Changes

This Privacy Statement was last revised on April 19th, 2021. In the instance a clause within this statement must me adjusted, changed or added this action may occur for any reason at any time. When our private policy has a significant change or addition all customers who are filed within our system will recieve an email to notify them of these changes. Also, Carefree Beauty will have a pop up window on our website 2 weeks following these updates to notify users. Before continuing shopping, customers will be required to check a box stating they have read, understood and agree with the changes made.

Choice

Carefree Beauty's main goal is to provide users with the best possible expereince. To fit the needs of all our customers we are proud to offer the opportunity for customers to provide information which will help our web server to get to know their individual likes and habits making our website easy to navigate and shop. With the element of choice we provide all customers the opportunity to avoid sharing personal information if that is what they desire and will make them feel the best while shopping.

Cookies and Opt Out Option

The Carefree Beauty website may use "cookies" to match a user with web content specific to individual users' interests like product suggestions. Cookies may also keep track of online purchasing transactions including how long a customer was shopping, what products they purchased and how often they visit the website and purchase products. Sensitive personal information including personal adresses, billing information and all infromation recieved through our secure checkout is not stored within cookies.

Customer Rights

Any of our customers can request for the deletion of any personal information collected by contacting our team through our designated privacy email or phone number. After a request is made for the deletion of any or all personal information from our data system, Carefree Beauty will reach out via email within 10 business days to confirm the deletion of personal information from your file or delete your file entirely. After CareFree Beauty processes the request to delete information, it will delete the information requested by the customer in a secure and irreversible manner to prevent the data from being restored. After a customer recieves a confirmation email that their data has been terminated from our system it has been fully removed.

Access

At Carefree Beauty, we value the customer and their information to better our own business and brand practices. We do so to fit the needs of our customers and provide an experience online that is secure and consistently improving. We hold the information our customers share within our website with care and only allow information to be used in the interest of benefiting the customer's individual experience as well as the expereinces of future customers. All information provided to Carefree Beauty is confidential and only provided to our Data Analysis and Marketing Teams, we do not share any information with outside parties. As stated before, all information provided to our Data Analysis and Marketing Teams does not have an attached identity to allow information to remain private.

What Data is used for

Information is collected by cookies enabled by the user, email signup for promotions, and placing an order. Our team is only allowed to view information given through the use of cookies or through pop up windows throughout the site that requests information such as email and birthday. All information within checkout is secure and confidential. With the information our team is provided, they will then follow up with researching two things with this information. First they will input the user into our customer files and second they will use this information as data to project future interactions with the customer and plan to better our website, products and brand to fit the needs of our customers. All information used is strategically important to analyze our current brand and business operations and better these practices to benefit our customers and expand our brand. Carefree Beauty does not associate any personal information to individual user identities. For example, by tracking a customer's activity on the Carefree Beauty website we are able to see what products customers are most interested in. With this information we can do a variety of things, first we can offer them other products they may be interested in, make products that may also be of interest to customers and predict what our customers are looking for to better their experience and create a sense of brand loyalty with the customer.
